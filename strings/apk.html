Apk cannot be installed This can have multiple reasons: Insufficient storage Your storage may be insufficient. Please make sure that you have at least 500MB plus the size of your APK available. Wrong Android version The Android SDK version required by the App may be higher than what your device currently runs on. Wrong device Maybe the APK was made for a specific device only, meaning that some configurations or resources for your specific device might not be available. App already installed Maybe some app blocks the installation. This could also be if the installed App has a higher version than the one you want to install. In this case, you may want to firstly uninstall current App, then reinstall yours.
