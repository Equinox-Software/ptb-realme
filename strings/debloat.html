Debloat There's two major ways of having a device debloated: flashing a debloated Rom or rooting your device and uninstalling things yourself. These methods void your warranty and can be risky! If you just want some free space or block apps from running, try /cleaners The alternative solution 1. Install ADB on your Computer XDA's Guide 2. Enable USB-Debugging on your device, plug your phone into the Computer 3. Open the command prompt and type in adb shell and then adb devices and make sure yours is listed there. Make sure you know exactly what Application you want to remove! Some are required by the system and might make it unstable or result in a crash. 4.
