Configurations Device specific configurations lead to better image quality. You can get configurations from Urnyx05's page. These configurations are optimized for a specific device, but may work for other devices aswell. Just give them a try 😊 To enable these configurations, place them in Internal Storage > GCam > Configs7. Then go to your GCam and press on the bottom left (next to the camera switch button) a few times. A dialog should appear where your can select the desired configuration. Also feel free to fiddle around with LibPatcher (in GCam's settings) a little to shape the image output so that it fits your needs.
